# Template 6 — Meeting Transcript

Use when a call/meeting transcript or notes have been pasted/discussed. Distill decisions + action items.

```
---
id: <date>-overton-meeting-<slug>
created: <ISO-8601>
scenario: meeting
title: <short title>
project: <repo basename or "none">
session_id: <CLAUDE_SESSION_ID>
status: in-progress|blocked|complete
focus: <verbatim focus, omit if none>
tags: [<keywords>]
next_step: <one-line>
related: []
---
```

# <Title> — Overton Snapshot (Meeting)

## Primary Intent
<Purpose of the meeting and why this snapshot exists. Front-loaded.>

## Attendees & Context
<Who was present (or speakers in the transcript) and the meeting's setting/agenda.>

## Key Points Raised
<The substantive discussion points, grouped by topic. Keep speaker attribution where it matters.>
- <point> — <speaker, if relevant>

## Decisions
<What was decided. Be unambiguous.>
- <decision>

## Action Items
<Each with owner and due date if stated.>
- [ ] <action> — **owner:** <name> · **due:** <date or TBD>

## Quotes Worth Preserving (verbatim)
<Direct quotes that capture commitments, constraints, or nuance that would be lost in paraphrase.>

## Follow-ups / Open Items
<Topics deferred or needing a future discussion.>

## Next Step (verbatim)
<The immediate next action. Include a direct quote of the most recent relevant statement.>
